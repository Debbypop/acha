ABOUT US

Acha Global Hub (AGH) is a licensed FX and cross-border payment solutions provider transforming how individuals and businesses move money across West Africa and beyond. With a strong presence in Nigeria, Ghana, and key global payment corridors, we deliver secure, fast, and cost-effective remittance and foreign exchange services across West Africa.

Our robust platform and network of trusted partners enable seamless cross-border transfers with reduced fees and minimal friction.

At AGH, we seek to redefine the remittance experience by simplifying global payments, supporting economic growth, and driving more efficient money movement across borders.
